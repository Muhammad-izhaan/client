About Us

Zivahh is a women-owned staffing and recruitment company based in the US. We specialize in connecting businesses with talented professionals for temporary, temp-to-perm, and permanent positions.

At Zivahh, we are committed to promoting diversity, inclusion, and equity in the workplace. Our rigorous screening and selection process ensures the best fit for your organization, and we take a personalized approach to recruitment to find candidates who align with your values.
